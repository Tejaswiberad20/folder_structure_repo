Chicken Chow Mein (SO EASY!)

If you are looking for an easy but delicious dinner, try making my Chicken Chow Mein

Ingredients

  • 450g of Egg Chow Mein Noodles
  • ½ Carrot (sliced)
  • ¼ Cabbage (sliced)
  • 10 Shiitake Mushroom (sliced)
  • 5 stalks of Celery (cut into thin strips)
  • ½ Large Onion (sliced)
  • 3 Stalks of Green Onion (2-inch pieces)

How to Make Chow Mein:

  1. In a small mixing bowl, combine ingredients for chow mein sauce: oyster sauce, sesame oil, soy sauce, chicken broth, and cornstarch. Set aside.
  2. Cook noodles according to package instructions, set aside.
  3. Heat a large wok or pan. Add a bit of oil to the pan and cook chicken until it’s golden brown. Remove chicken and set aside.
  4. Add carrots, cabbage and pressed garlic and saute for a few minutes until veggies are slightly softened.
  5. Add chicken back to the pan, followed by cooked noodles and pour the sauce right on top.
  6. Cook for about 2 minutes, distributing all that saucy goodness around evenly.
  7. Add chopped green onions and remove from the heat. Serve hot!
